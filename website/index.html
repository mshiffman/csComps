Project Overview

Our project is a survey of community detection algorithms and their application to the creation of public transportation lines and networks. Using a U.S. census dataset of origin-destination data in Hennepin County, MN, we created a graph linking each census tract in the county, with edges between tracts weighted equal to the number of people who live in one and work in another.
